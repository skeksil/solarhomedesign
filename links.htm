Erwin is also an is an Australian artist well known for his paintings and drawings of Australia's wilderness areas and cultural heritage. www.erwinweberart.com.au


Erwins Art has been exhibited in conjunction with and raising funds for the following non government and government organisations:

Australian Conservation Foundation
New South Wales National Parks and Wildlife Service (Now DECC)
Fraser Island Defenders Organisation (FIDO)
Many of the Places painted by Erwin are now inscribed to be protected for all humanity on the UNESCO World Heritage List
